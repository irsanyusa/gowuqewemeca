Justin Bieber Believed That Marriage Would Fix His Problems, but This Was Not the Case

June 2024 · 4 minute read

When you’re young, fame can affect how you perceive the world and the people in it. Justin Bieber has been open in recent years about his battles with trauma and mental health. But the singer proved he could overcome whatever brought him to his knees, and he is now headed toward a more promising future.

Bright Side is a firm believer in the power of perseverance and love from those closest to you to help you overcome life’s challenges, and we are confident that Justin will heal with Hailey’s love by his side.

He rose to prominence when he was only 13 years old.

PHOTOlink/Courtesy Everett Collection/East News

Since his first hit, Baby, which catapulted him to the heights of fame, Justin has spent the last 12 years living under the constant scrutiny of millions of fans and media attention. “I went from a 13-year-old boy from a small town to being praised left and right by the world, with millions saying how much they loved me and how great I was,” he declared.

While he was still a teenager, Justin’s fame grew year after year, with the release of additional albums like My World 2.0 and Believe, as well as international tours that earned him millions of dollars. He was aging and transforming in front of everyone, both mentally and physically. Because of his enormous fame, the mainstream media has always closely followed his every move and slip-up.

His love story with Selena Gomez

Meanwhile, the well-known Canadian singer was deeply involved in an emotional relationship with actress and singer Selena Gomez. Many of Justin’s songs were devoted to her, and the couple was both adored and scrutinized by everyone. Because of their high-profile relationship, with many rumors and scandals around it, Selena and Justin’s relationship ended up being an on-and-off relationship for a while.

The couple battled for years to keep their union intact before breaking up in 2014. Over the years, Selena and Justin have dated a variety of people and in 2015, Justin said, “We were so in love. Nothing else mattered. We were all about each other.”

In the midst of all this on-again, off-again, Justin and Hailey were flirting at some point, but they never took things further.

Miguel Aguilar, PacificCoastNews/EAST NEWS

In 2018, Justin and Selena reconnected once more, and people saw them out and about. However, the reconciliation only lasted a few months because they split up and that’s when Justin started dating Hailey.

Justin tied the knot and took a step out of the spotlight.

In June 2018, when Justin and Hailey reconnected, it was “love at first sight.” They started to appear in front of many people and could be seen cuddling everywhere. While they were on vacation in the Bahamas, Justin proposed to her. The singer announced their engagement on Instagram, referring to Hailey as the “love of his life.”

They first exchanged vows in secret a few months after the proposal in a courtroom in New York City, but on October 7, 2019, they did so once more in a formal ceremony. According to some sources, the wedding had to be postponed because of Justin’s health issues. “It was months of me being a new wife trying to help him figure out what was wrong and what was going on,” Hailey admitted.

In 2019, in order to devote more time to himself and his mental health, Justin announced he would step away from music and the spotlight for a while. He wrote in a caption on Instagram for his followers: “I don’t deserve that, and you don’t deserve that, you pay money to come and have a lively energetic fun light concert, and I was unable emotionally to give you that.”

Earlier that year, the singer opened up about his depression battle, admitting he had struggled for a while. Bieber first encountered these issues, he claims, during his Purpose tour in 2017. “I was lonely. I needed some time. ”

The emotional breakdown post-marriage

Due to health concerns, Justin recently canceled the remainder of his tour, which was slated to take place in June and July 2022. He is now taking care of himself with the guidance of his wife. However, the musician recently talked openly about the emotional fall he experienced shortly after marrying Hailey Baldwin.

Hiding nothing, Justin says that his marriage has pushed him to improve himself and face his past trauma. Even though Justin and Hailey were deeply in love, Bieber understood that getting married would not end all of his problems from the past. He was forthright and truthful in an interview. “I had a bit of an emotional breakdown because I thought marriage was going to fix all of my problems.”

The 28-year-old musician had a completely different perception of himself and expected his wife to “do something you’re not doing.” Justin recently embarked on a new path that is assisting him in improving and accepting himself for who he is: “Day by day, I get to just get better and better and not be too hard on myself.”

Despite the challenges, the adorable newlyweds never stopped sharing and spreading their love and helping one another out in good times and bad. Compromises and sacrifices are a part of marriage, and Justin and Haley’s love has been strong enough to endure them all. Hailey had stated, “I know for a fact that I’ve loved this person for a very long time, and now would not be the time to give up on him.”
