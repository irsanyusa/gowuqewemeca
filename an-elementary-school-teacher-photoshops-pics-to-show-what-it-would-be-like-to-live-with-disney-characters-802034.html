Having friends who encourage us and make us smile — and with whom we can share the most important moments — is perhaps one of the best things life has to offer. At the same time, respecting each person’s time and individuality is a kind of “gift” that not everyone possesses. Therefore, if you have friends who respect you, you should enjoy them to the fullest.
