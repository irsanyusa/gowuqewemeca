I Can See Your Voice is returning for Season 3, and the show is filming this week in Georgia. Celebrity panelist Adrienne Bailon recently posted on Instagram that she was back on the set. Fans can get tickets to the show’s tapings for free.

I Can See Your Voice Season 3 Starts Filming

I Can See Your Voice premiered in 2020 on FOX, hosted by Ken Jeong. The premise of the show is that contestants must determine whether someone is a good or bad singer without hearing them sing live. They’re assisted by celebrity detectives, including regular panelists Adrienne Bailon and Cheryl Hines.

Bailon recently posted on Instagram stories that she was back on set for the next season. Tickets are available now for fans to witness an upcoming taping in person. Current dates listed are November 2, 4, 8, and 10. There will be two tapings per day, at 9 a.m. and 3 p.m.

Tapings take place at Trilith Studios in Fayetteville, Georgia. Attendees must be at least 18 years old. According to the ticket website, Covid-19 vaccinations are not required, but audience members will be rapid tested before entry.

How to Sign Up to Be on the Show

If simply being an audience member doesn’t cut it for you, and you want to participate in the show itself, you can sign up to be considered as a contestant or a singer. Those who think they can guess whether someone is a good singer can sign up here.

If you want to appear on the show as a singer, you can sign up here. You’ll be asked to specify if you’re a good or a bad singer before going forward. You might hear back from a casting producer about appearing on the show.

I Can See Your Voice Season 2 wrapped up in March of this year, with a special airing in June. As of right now, a release date has yet to be announced for the upcoming third season.
