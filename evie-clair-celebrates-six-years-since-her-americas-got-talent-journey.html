Evie Clair Celebrates Six Years Since Her 'Americas Got Talent' Journey

June 2024 · 2 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Evie Clair recently reminisced about her memorable journey during America’s Got Talent season 12. The Missouri-based musician was a fan-favorite contestant six years back, with her amazing piano skills and angelic vocals.

Evie Clair Reflects on AGT Experience Six Years After Her Run

In an Instagram post dated August 2, the viral YouTube sensation shared her thoughts over the pleasant memory. Admitting that she had mixed emotions during the time, Clair is, overall, thankful for the show.

“It feels sort of crazy to me that it’s been six years since my time on @agt,” she wrote. “It all feels a little bit like a distant dream now. lots of both good and hard feelings… but i will always look back on this time with gratitude.”

Alongside the caption, Clair shared a series of snaps recounting her time in the competition. Such included a photo from her audition performance and several pictures from her live show singing acts.

In the comment section, fans were also quick to join the singer in recollecting her season 12 memories. One follower particularly remembered “crying my eyes out” during her audition. Meanwhile, another supported stated how they’re still able to get “goosebumps” every time they hear her voice.

She Shared a Little Life Update to Fans

Almost a week ago, Clair gave fans a glimpse of what was happening to her a month back. Clearly, the AGT singer had an eventful previous days.

“These past few weeks have been CRAZY but so fun,” she wrote. 

Giving a rundown of her recent life experiences through pictures, the singer announced that she and her husband Clancy Thomas graduated together. Their family also celebrated Thomas’ 23rd birthday.

Apparently, she and her baby daughter Billye took a trip to Arizona, seized some road trip, and met with other relatives. In the same update, Clair declared that they officially “got settled in to our NEW HOME.”

“Yep, we moved!!! hi Missouri! humidity is crazy. we have a lot of adjusting to do. but we love it here already and we are so excited for this new adventure!” she wrote.

 Amid fateful happenings in their lives, Clair stated that their family feels “so blessed.” In another post, the singer expressed gratitude for July 2023 — for being a crazy month.
