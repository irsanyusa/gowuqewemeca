Mickey Mantle (Baseball Legend) - On This Day

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Profession: Baseball Legend

Biography: Regarded by many as one of the greatest players in baseball history.

Mantle played center fielder and first baseman for the New York Yankees for 18 seasons, from 1951 through 1968.

He appeared in 12 World Series, his team winning 7 of them, and holds numerous World Series batting records.

Born: October 20, 1931
Birthplace: Spavinaw, Oklahoma, USA

Generation: Silent Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Goat/Sheep
Star Sign: Libra

Died: August 13, 1995 (aged 63)
Cause of Death: Cancer
