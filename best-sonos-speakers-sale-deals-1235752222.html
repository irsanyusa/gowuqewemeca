The portable speaker has become an instant must-have among tech junkies and audiophiles alike, for its big, bold sound, easy pairing and sleek, streamlined design.

Sonos is also offering 20% off its Era 100 smart speaker, which delivers rich, full sound, with deep bass and crisp highs. Sonos’ “Trueplay” technology automatically adjusts the sound based on your environment and the type of music you’re listening to, so tunes always come out crystal clear.
