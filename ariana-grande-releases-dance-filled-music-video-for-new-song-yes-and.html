Ariana Grande is back with new music. On Friday, the singer released a new single titled “Yes, And?” The song comes with a new music video, which features Grande showing off a stylish dance routine for a group of critics.

Ariana Grande Releases ‘Yes, And?’ Music Video

Grande’s new song has a positive message about moving forward through tough situations. As she sings on the chorus, “‘Yes, and?’ / Say that sh*t with your chest, and / Be your own f*ckin’ best friend / Say that sh*t with your chest / Keep moving like, ‘What’s next?’ / ‘Yes, and?'”

The music video begins with a group of critics arriving at an event and gossiping about Grande. They enter a large room that contains several statues, including one of the singer herself. The statues crumble, and Grande appears with several backup dancers to perform a routine set to the song.

The critics end up getting into the song, with some of them even joining in on the dance. At the end of the video, Grande disappears, and the critics notice that her statue is back. They leave as a new group of critics enters the room.

Fans Are Excited for Grande’s New Album

Grande’s new song is the first peek fans have gotten at the singer’s upcoming seventh studio album — her first new project since 2020’s Positions. The singer hasn’t confirmed the album’s title yet, but she did share what is apparently one of several album covers.

Fans have also been speculating about what the album might be called. Based on the coordinates on an invitation card in the “Yes, And?” music video, some fans have pointed to Montauk, New York. That’s a significant location in the movie Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind.

Grande seemed to confirm the connection by commenting a heart emoji on a clip from the movie. Will the singer’s new album be called Eternal Sunshine? Hopefully we’ll know for sure soon.
