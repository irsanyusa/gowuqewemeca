Where to Buy the Barbie Soundtrack on Vinyl

August 2024 · 4 minute read

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

Billie Eilish and Finneas O’Connell won the Oscar Sunday night for “What Was I Made For?,” their contribution to the “Barbie” soundtrack.

Greta Gerwig‘s neon pink blockbuster received nine total Oscar nominations this year, including two that competed for Best Song (“I’m Just Ken” didn’t win the trophy but got an electric performance by Ryan Gosling at the ceremony).

The 17-track “Barbie,” which includes “What Was I Made For?,” “I’m Just Ken” and Dua Lip’a’s chart-topping “Dance the Night Away,” is available to purchase on digital, CD and vinyl on Amazon.

Ahead of the film, Eilish released a music video for her soundtrack single “What Was I Made For?” In the self-directed video, the pop star is seen dressed as a polished and pensive doll while she places a variety of Barbie outfits — replicas of her own looks throughout the years — onto a mini clothing hanger.
